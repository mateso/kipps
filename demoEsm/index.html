Kibaha Independent Pre & Primary School (KIPS)

EDUCATION IS LIGHT


Overview

Kibaha Independent Pre and Primary School (KIPS) is one of the best schools in providing quality education for pre and primary school students. Located at Kibaha - coast region about 35 kilometers from Dar es salaam, the school has both day and boarding facilities.

Classrooms

The school has well ventilated classrooms that can accommodate 30 to 35 students.
The school teachers are qualified, well trained and eager to educate our pupils, hence enlightening the future generation.

Computer Lab.

The school has a computer lab with enough number of computers in ratio of one computer to one student, this ensures that all our students have a chance of getting computer knowledge which help them cope with development of science and technology.

Dormitory

The school has two dormitories one for boys and another for girls. The total accommodation capacity of the dormitory is 70 students.

School Transport

The school has two well maintained mini buses that are effectively used for transporting day students to and from school.

  • ADMISSION

    The school admits students of all races, gender, religion, culture and nationalities. The prime source of students is Kibaha and neighbouring regions. Students from other regions are admitted in boarding section.

    Registration forms are available at the school campus Kibaha Mkoani and at Njuweni Institute offices, Mwembe Chai PLAZA, Dar es salaam.
