Prof. Tolly Mbwette
Civil & Environment Eng.

Prof. Tolly Mbwette is a Professor of Environmental Science and Technology of the Open University of Tanzania (OUT) as well as a Professor of Environmental Engineering of the University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM). He is also a former Vice Chancellor of the Open University of Tanzania (OUT). Professionally, Professor Mbwette has a long list of intarnational and regional credentials including extensive consulting and research. He obtained his PhD in Civil Engineering (1989) at the University of London UK, His Masters degree in Civil Engineering was obtained at the University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM) in 1984. He earned a postgraduate Diploma in Sanitary Engineering with a Distinction in 1982, IHE Delft, the Netherlands. He obtained Bachelor Degree (Civil Engineering) in 1981 at the University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Prof. Tolly Mbwette has extensively conducted research and consultancy in the areas of Environmental Engineering, ICT, Institutional Reforms and Environmental Management.
