Welcome to Mbwette Professorial Library

A Fountain of Literacy and Scientific Research

Overview

Mbwette Professorial Library offers vast collection of research and reading materials. The Library is a home to the collection of published and unpublished intellectual outputs emanated from Prof. Tolly Mbwette's works and his relatives. The collection consists of manuscripts, research reports, consultancy reports, conference proceedings, published and unpublished papers and rare books. A collection of videos and scanned pictures is also available.

The Library maintains both digital and printed collection of published and unpublished resources available. Access to printed collection is mainly facilitated through Library Management Information System (koha) as well as an index of the available printed resources, while access to digital collection is facilitated by E- Print system. The Library is responsible for directly supporting the national and international research community.

We welcome all interested researchers to expolore and enjoy the unique collection held by the Tolly Mbwette Professorial Library.
