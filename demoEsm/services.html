Services

Mbwette Professorial Library Services

Library Catalogue

The Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) used in the Library is called KOHA. The system is mainly used to locate printed collection of the available resources. In the OPAC page a collection can be browsed by either TITLE, AUTHOR, SUBJECT or by CALL NUMBER.


Institutional Repository

The Institutional Repository system used in the Library is called EPrint. The system is used to access digital collection of both published and unpublished resources available in the Library. The collection can be browsed by either TITLE, AUTHOR or SUBJECT.


Becoming a member

Membership and access to its services is open to all Undergraduates, Graduate students, Academic and Researchers. We welcome all interested researchers to use the Library. All reseachers need to register on their first visit so that we can issue a Research Library Card. On your first visit, please allow 5 minutes to complete the process of registration with a member of our staff. You will be asked to complete a registation form and degree form and agree to abide by the regulations governing use of the Library


Membership Fees

Library Regulation

  2. Smoking is strictly forbidden
  3. Food or drinks are not allowed in the reading rooms
  4. Use of cellular phones in the reading rooms is not allowed
  5. Defacing, marking or furnishing of books and other information materials is strictly forbidden.
